# Product Personas: TranscriptAI

Based on the feature set (Offline Transcription, Global Dictation, MLX Optimization) and the Feature Roadmap, here are the primary target users for TranscriptAI.

## 1. The Privacy-Conscious Professional (Primary)
**Who they are:** Lawyers, Doctors, Journalists, Therapists, Executives.
**Pain Point:** They need to transcribe sensitive meetings, patient notes, or interviews but **cannot** upload audio to the cloud (Otter.ai, OpenAI, etc.) due to NDA, HIPAA, or strict data privacy laws.
**Why TranscriptAI:**
- **100% Offline Processing:** Audio never leaves their machine.
- **Security:** No data leaks, no third-party server access.
- **No Subscriptions:** One-time purchase aligns with software procurement policies.

## 2. The Student / Researcher (High Volume)
**Who they are:** University students (Med, Law, STEM), PhD researchers.
**Pain Point:** Drowning in hours of recorded lectures and interviews. Need to search through audio to find specific concepts or quotes. Cloud services are too expensive for 40+ hours/month.
**Why TranscriptAI:**
- **Unlimited Transcription:** No per-minute cost.
- **Speed:** Transcribe a 1-hour lecture in minutes on M1/M2/M3 Macs.
- **Study Tools:** (Planned) summarization and flashcard generation.

## 3. The Content Creator (Speed & Workflow)
**Who they are:** YouTubers, Podcasters, Writers, Bloggers.
**Pain Point:** Need to turn video/audio into subtitles, blog posts, or show notes quickly. Global dictation is faster than typing drafts.
**Why TranscriptAI:**
- **Global Dictation:** Write scripts/emails into *any* app (Obsidian, Scrivener, Notion) using voice.
- **YouTube Support:** (Planned) Direct URL transcription for researching competitors or repurposing content.
- **Diarization:** Speaker labeling for podcast editing.

## 4. The Tech-Savvy Efficiency Hacker
**Who they are:** Developers, Product Managers, "Power Users".
**Pain Point:** Loves automating workflows. Wants to utilize the full power of their expensive Apple Silicon hardware. Hates subscription fatigue.
**Why TranscriptAI:**
- **MLX Optimization:** Validates their hardware investment.
- **Hackability:** Local files, potential for scripting/automating with the generated text.
- **No "Checking Speech Model" Hangups:** Instant-on performance (once optimized).

## 5. The Meeting Heavy Freelancer
**Who they are:** Consultants, Project Managers, Virtual Assistants.
**Pain Point:** Back-to-back meetings. Losing track of action items and decisions.
**Why TranscriptAI:**
- **Automated Minutes:** (Planned) Auto-summaries and action item extraction.
- **Archive:** Searchable history of "what did the client say three weeks ago?".

---

## Value Prop Summary
**"Unleash your Mac's power. Keep your data. Transcribe everything."**
TranscriptAI bridges the gap between high-quality AI transcription (Whisper) and privacy/usability, specifically targeting Apple Silicon users who are underserved by cloud-only web apps.
